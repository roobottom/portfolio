Natasha Bailie

Summer 2008
For Natasha Bailie Vintage Clothing Co.
My role Website design & build
Team Me and Natasha

Natasha loves anything vintage, but most especially dresses, she’s nutty about them. She asked me to create an e-commerce site for her fledgling dress company.

Natasha and her husband Ian were really great to work with. They wanted the look and feel of the site to reflect Natasha’s style, and brought their combined marketing and fashion expertise to the mix. I brought my design skills and technical expertise in interpreting their requirements.

The result was a website that was a massive hit with buyer and fashion writers alike. Natasha’s online store was even featured in Vogue.
